Press Release

John Allsopp, Jina Bolton, Eric Clark Su, & other web design superheroes are landing in Manila, Philippines for the 5th Form Function & Class web design conference.

Manila, Philippines – Form Function & Class is an annual gathering that imparts essential knowledge to the web design community. FFC will be held on November 8, 2014, marking its fifth year as the pioneering event for web designers in Asia.

The conference features an all-star lineup of industry experts, led by:

John Allsopp – founder of the Web Directions conferences, author of “A Dao of Web Design”, hailed as the “manifesto for anyone working on the Web”;

Jina Bolton – senior product designer for UX at Salesforce.com, authored Fancy Form Design and The Art & Science of CSS, leads the Sass design team, previously at Apple, GitHub, Engine Yard, Crush+Lovely;

Eric Clark Su – co-founder of Lifebit, has shipped over 20 mobile apps for some of the Philippines’ top brands, including Microsoft PH, Nokia PH, Axe, and Coke.

FFC is open to all web practitioners, designers & developers, professionals & students, freelancers & hobbyists, makers of web & mobile sites/interfaces/apps. Whether you’re just starting to learn web design or are a veteran of the industry, you should make sure that your skills in design, front-end, HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, UX are up to date & up to par.

Are you doing web design right? Are you doing it well? Are you making the Web a better place, or are you going to destroy it?

To register, visit http://formfunctionclass.com/. The Early Bird rate of PhP3,000 will end on September 26. Student discounts are available with schools of the partner organizations: UST TomasinoWeb, Ateneo GRIDS, and La Salle Computer Society. Interested in attending both FFC and UX Philippines? Grab the FFC+UXPH Superpass for only PhP 8,000.

For any further inquiries, you may contact info@pwdo.org.

Form Function & Class is organized by the Philippine Web Designers Organization (PWDO), a grassroots group of web professionals who want to create a venue for meeting and learning from the best minds in the industry.

FFC is PWDO’s culminating event for the year, following the sophomore year of its site-building hackathon for charities & non-profit organizations called Caritakathon, as well as the monthly MiniFFC web designer meetups.

The conference is supported by Microsoft, PunchDrunk Panda, Adobe, Elance-oDesk, Offscreen Magazine, Campaign Monitor, Mozilla Firefox, Packt Publishing, A Book Apart, Code School, Google Developers, Developers Connect Philippines, & UX Philippines.

About

<form> function() & .class is an annual conference that imparts essential knowledge to the web design community in Asia and the rest of the world, from the beautiful tropical shores of the Philippines.

FFC not only highlights the best practices of crafting websites from desktop to mobile devices, but also aims to inculcate values of quality and excellence in the web design profession. Sessions cover HTML, CSS, JS, typography, user experience, mobile, business, and other relevant disciplines.

Since its launch in 2009, the conference has gathered hundreds of industry practitioners, educators, students, and hobbyists every year. It has seen speakers from Mozilla, Google, Adobe, as well as the brightest minds in the country and the region. This November 8, FFC will celebrate its 5th year of promoting web standards, accessibility, usability, professionalism, and excellence.

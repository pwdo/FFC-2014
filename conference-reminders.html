Conference Reminders

Ready for this year’s Form Function & Class web design conference? Here are some very important reminders you need to remember, so please read carefully:

Schedule

Registration starts at 8:00 am. The programme will run from 9:00 am to 6:00 pm, followed by an after-party later in the evening (details on this to be announced).

Attire

The following are prohibited clothing inside the campus: tube tops, tank tops, backless tops, cleavage-baring tops, see-through garments, short shorts/dresses/skirts, open toe footwear that do not have a back-strap/slingback or heels that are at least 1 inch. Please refer to the handbook for more details.

Venue

FFC will be held at 4th Floor Multipurpose Room, Henry Sy Hall, De La Salle University. Please enter at the Henry Sy Hall gate, present a valid ID to security, and have a copy of your ticket ready (digital or print) in case they ask for it.

Parking

Off-campus paid parking is available. You can refer to the purple markers on this map.

Hotels

Nearby hotels you can stay at are: Taft Tower Hotel Manila, Hotel Benilde, and Century Park Hotel. You can refer to the red markers on this map.

Receipts

Official receipts will be issued to those who requested them in their ticket details. Please make sure the company, TIN, and total amount are listed to avoid delays. (Recommended: if your company purchased multiple tickets, you might want to have just one person requesting this, indicating the combined amount.)

Certificates

Digital certificates will be emailed to those who requested them in their ticket details after the conference.

Devices

If you’re bringing in any other devices besides laptops, please email us together with your ticketholder name.

Food

We’ll serve morning & afternoon snacks with coffee, as well as lunch.

Conduct

FFC is anti-harrassment, anti-discrimination. Let’s make this conference an awesome experience for everyone :)

Social Media

Official hashtag for the event is #FFCPH. You can also use these images to post about FFC.

See you on November 8!
